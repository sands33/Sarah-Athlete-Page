About Me

I began standup paddleboarding in the waters around Seattle in 2013. I am drawn to the easy access to the waters of Puget Sound, as well as the physical challenge and competition of racing. I feel fortunate to have met so many fantastic people in the SUP community and to have found a passion in this growing sport.

A fisheries biologist by profession, I try to spend as much time on the water as possible. In addition to standup paddleboarding, I enjoy surfing, kiteboarding, whitewater kayaking, and outrigger canoeing. Thanks for visiting, and I hope to see you on the water soon!
