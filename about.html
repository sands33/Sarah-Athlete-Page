All about myself

I am originally a Minnesotan who first came to the Great Pacific Northwest in 2005 for a Masters in Fisheries Sciences at the University of Washington. Before living in Seattle I worked as a Biologist and a Marine Science Instructor in California. There I learned to surf and SUP. I began SUP Racing at Urban Surf's weekly Summer SUP Series, then graduated to competing in bigger regional races, like Round the Rock, and (my favorite) the Naish Columbia Gorge Downwind Paddle Challenge. You will find me stand up paddling in Shilshole Bay; I often launch from the Ballard Elk's Club parking lot- across from my favorite sandwich shopPaseo Cuban Restaurant. My newest training activity is paddling with theSand Point Outrigger Club.I am grateful to my newest sponsor,KIALOA PADDLES, plus to my friends, family, and to other local paddlers for their support. I live in Seattle with my husband and two dogs who will get you if you mess with me. Thanks toMonster and Seafor the sweet padding jersey that you see me wearing in this picture from the Summer of 2014. Check myblog often for local SUP news and please support my sponsors by clicking on their links. See you on the water!
