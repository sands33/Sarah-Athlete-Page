About Me

I try to spend as much time on the water as possible. I am a fisheries biologist by profession. I enjoy surfing, standup paddleboarding (SUPing), kiteboarding, and whitewater kayaking. Most recently, I have started paddling with theSand Point Outrigger Canoe Club.

I began SUP Racing in 2013 at Urban Surf's weekly Summer SUP Series. I quickly became hooked. I feel fortunate to have met so many fantastic people in the northwest SUP community, and to have found a passion in this growing sport. I am especially grateful to my newest sponsor KIALOA Paddles, and to Minimum Wake Consortium for creating this website. Check myblog often for local SUP news and please support my sponsors by clicking on their links. See you on the water!
