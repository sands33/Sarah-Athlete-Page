Newest sponsor: KIALOA Paddles

I got a call from pro SUP'er Beau Whitehead about being on the local KIALOA SUP team. I had previously used some KIALOA paddles while outrigger canoe cross-training, so I already knew that KIALOA would make a great SUP paddle too. Since then KIALOA and I have spoken about adding a 3 piece travel paddle to the line, and it looks like that is going to happen!
